The Land of the Bengal

Bangladesh is a country located in South Asia. My parents were born and raised there and I also lived there for 3 years. It's a nice country with a lot of great people and lots of culture.

You only have one life so you should live it up to the fullest. If you do end up going there you will have a tremendous time getting to know the people as well as getting new memories that will stick with you forever.

About Bangladesh

Bangladesh, also known as the Land of the Bengal, was once a part of British India until the nation gained independence in 1947. It broke off from Pakistan in 1971 in a long and bloody confilct and is now one of the fastest growing economies in the developing world. Despite its constant evolution the nation retains its own culture and the people have their own distinct lifestyle. From the crowded megacities to the rural areas, this nation is a sight to behold.

The Animal Kingdom

This nation is full of wonderfull creatres of all shapes and sizes. Just about every harmless crickets to scaly reptile exists somewhere in the countryside. The Bengal Tiger remains the apex predator in the heavily dense sundurban region. According to the World Wildlife Fund, these tigers are officially an endangered species. Because of that, it it is illegal to try to find or hunt them by yourself - not that anyone would try to face a Bengal Tiger by themselves.
