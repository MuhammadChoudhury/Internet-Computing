To Do List

Check or Uncheck

You can check an item on the list simply by clicking on it. Clicking on it again will uncheck it. You can also click on the "Check/Uncheck All" button to Check or Uncheck all items on the list.

Add and Delete

Type the item that you want on your list in the input bar. You can delete an item by double clicking on an item. Double-clicking on the "Check/
Uncheck" button will delete all items present on the list.

Edit

Tapping on the "Edit Mode" will enable you to edit any item on the list. Double-clicking on the button will disable edit mode. And don't worry, your list won't be deleted even if you refresh the page.
